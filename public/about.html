Guests/Registered Users-

As a user of GetMeSafe, you may use it as a guest user or a registered user. A registered user will have the capability of adding thier emergency contacts, who will be contacted when you request to use our app.

Emergency contacts -

If you are an emergency contact for a user of GetMeSafe, we will send you an email if/when your friend requests to use the GetMeSafe app. The email will have an url to a map to tell where you friend is and the route they are following. We have implemented SMS notifications into our app, but they are still in testing and not currently available to all users.
